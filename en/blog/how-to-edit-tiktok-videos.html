üéØ Essential Editing Techniques

1. Jump Cuts

Cut out pauses and filler words. Keep the pace fast. Modern viewers have short attention spans - don't waste their time.

2. Text on Screen

Add text to reinforce key points. Many watch without sound, so text ensures your message gets across.

3. Speed Ramping

Speed up boring parts, slow down impactful moments. Creates dynamic pacing that keeps viewers engaged.

4. Music Sync

Match your cuts to the beat. Sound transitions on bass drops. This makes videos feel polished and satisfying.

5. Transitions

Use transitions sparingly. Match cut (movement continues), snap transitions, and simple fades work best. Avoid cheesy effects.
