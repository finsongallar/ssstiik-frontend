Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle your data.

Last updated: December 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Download Any TikTok ("we", "our" or "site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our TikTok video download service.

2. Information We Collect

Our service is designed to collect minimal necessary information:

  • Video URLs: We temporarily collect the TikTok links you enter to process the download. These links are not stored permanently.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous information about how you use our site, such as pages visited and time spent.
  • Technical information: Browser type, operating system and IP address (anonymized) for security and analysis purposes.

3. What We DON'T Collect

For your peace of mind, we inform you that we DO NOT collect:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, address, phone)
  • Login credentials for TikTok or any other platform
  • The videos you download (we don't store copies)
  • Download history linked to specific users
  • Payment information (our service is free)

4. How We Use Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Process your video download requests
  • Improve and optimize our service
  • Detect and prevent abuse or misuse
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our site may use cookies to:

  • Remember your usage preferences
  • Analyze site traffic (Google Analytics)
  • Display relevant ads (Google AdSense)

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect some site features.

6. Advertising

We use third-party advertising services, such as Google AdSense, to display ads on our site. These services may use cookies to display ads based on previous visits to this and other sites. You can disable personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or court order
  • To protect our legal rights
  • With service providers who assist us (hosting, analytics), under strict confidentiality agreements

8. Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure connection via HTTPS
  • Protected and monitored servers
  • Restricted access to information

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you
  • Request correction of incorrect information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us through our contact page.

10. Minors

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not intentionally collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through a notice on our site. We recommend that you review this page regularly.
